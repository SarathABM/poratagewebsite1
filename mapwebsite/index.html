About MAP

Kerala Heritage in Manitoba

Bringing the beauty and traditions of God's Own Country to the Canadian prairies

The Malayali Association of Portage la Prairie is a nonprofit, community-led organization dedicated to supporting and celebrating Malayali culture in Portage la Prairie, Manitoba. Through vibrant events, educational programs, and community services, we create a bridge between our rich heritage and our Canadian home.

Our Mission

To preserve, promote, and share the beautiful traditions of Kerala while fostering a strong, supportive community in Manitoba.

Our Vision

A thriving Malayali community that celebrates cultural diversity while contributing meaningfully to Canadian society.

Our Values

Unity, cultural pride, community service, and the spirit of togetherness that defines the Malayali way of life.

What We Do

Our diverse programs and services strengthen community bonds while celebrating our heritage

Community Building

Regular gatherings, social events, and networking opportunities that bring our community together

Cultural Promotion

Dance performances, music concerts, and art exhibitions showcasing Kerala's rich cultural heritage

Youth & Family Engagement

Programs for children and families to learn Malayalam, traditional arts, and cultural values

Support & Services

Assistance with settlement, translation services, and support during important life events
